About Minziro

Minziro is a village northwest of Tanzania. It is about 120 square kilometers big? and has about 10 000 inhabitants.

The main sources of income are the crops coffee, bananas, banana beer and tomatoes. The average annual income per family is about 500?. Minziro is enclosed by a 6.4 km wide and dense jungle and difficult to access. There is no electricity, no running water and no hospital.

In the meantime, the primeval forest of Minziros is an attractive nature reserve with beautiful butterflies, Birds, trees and more. (http://www.nature-reserves.go.tz/fnr/minziro or https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Minziro_Forest_Reserve).
